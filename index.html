May of the photos that emerged from the era (especially the photos by Dorothea Lange) captured this. Its these elements that are reflected in the traits and backgrounds chosen for this season.

1970/80s New York

1970/80s New York was a hell of a place to be. People were flooding into the city to experience the fashion and music scene. People were marching because of the Vietnam war and vagrancy was on the rise. All of these are reflected in the carefully chosen traits for this season.
